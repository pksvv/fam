# Personas

## Persona 1: Audit Response Lead

### Role

Owns the end-to-end response for an audit case or set of IDRs.

### Goals

- Understand requests quickly and accurately
- Coordinate work across teams
- Ensure the final submission is complete and defensible
- Reduce time spent chasing status and searching for precedent

### Pain Points

- Requests are ambiguous and time-sensitive
- Manual coordination across many contributors is slow
- Hard to know whether evidence is truly sufficient
- Late-stage surprises create avoidable risk

### Product Needs

- Case dashboard with question status and blockers
- AI-generated response strategy with rationale
- Workflow orchestration and owner visibility
- Reviewable draft responses and package assembly

## Persona 2: Tax Subject Matter Reviewer

### Role

Reviews question interpretation, strategy, evidence adequacy, and response narratives for tax accuracy.

### Goals

- Confirm that the platform interprets IDRs correctly
- Reuse proven response patterns when appropriate
- Catch weak claims and high-risk phrasing before submission

### Pain Points

- Historical precedent is scattered
- Drafts often arrive without clear evidence linkage
- Risk and confidence are not surfaced early enough

### Product Needs

- Question-level interpretation and precedent view
- Clear evidence-to-claim traceability
- Ability to edit, approve, reject, or send back work
- Visibility into assumptions and confidence flags

## Persona 3: Evidence Owner / Business Contributor

### Role

Provides data, documents, reports, reconciliations, and contextual explanations needed to support responses.

### Goals

- Receive clear requests with explicit deliverables
- Know what evidence is required, by when, and why
- Avoid repeated back-and-forth for missing details

### Pain Points

- Requests are often vague or incomplete
- Ownership and dependencies are unclear
- Evidence gets uploaded without context or reuse

### Product Needs

- Well-defined tasks with expected outputs
- Evidence upload and metadata capture
- Visibility into due dates, dependencies, and review feedback

## Persona 4: Legal / Compliance Reviewer

### Role

Reviews high-sensitivity responses, supporting materials, and submission posture.

### Goals

- Ensure responses are defensible and appropriately framed
- Confirm required approvals and review steps were followed
- Reduce risk from unsupported or overbroad claims

### Pain Points

- Review often happens too late
- Source lineage is incomplete
- It is hard to distinguish approved evidence from draft support

### Product Needs

- Approval gates and controlled review workflow
- Immutable history of edits, decisions, and evidence versions
- Clear package view of what will be submitted

## Persona 5: Product / Audit Governance Owner

### Role

Owns adoption, controls, metrics, and evolution of the platform.

### Goals

- Prove value in the pilot
- Ensure safe and governed use of AI
- Build reusable capability for broader rollout

### Pain Points

- Hard to measure where time is lost today
- AI pilots often drift into narrow chat demos
- Governance requirements are discovered too late

### Product Needs

- Usage and outcome metrics
- Configurable controls and approval policies
- Visibility into ontology gaps, override patterns, and feedback signals
