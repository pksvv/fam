# Future Scope

## Capability Expansion

- Broader audit coverage across additional jurisdictions, authorities, and tax topics
- Richer ontology coverage for more entities, systems, evidence classes, and response patterns
- More advanced contradiction analysis and multi-source evidence reasoning
- Deeper reviewer guidance based on prior acceptance outcomes and governance policy

## Integration Expansion

- Enterprise system integrations for ERP, GL, data warehouse, content repositories, workpaper systems, and email archives
- Automated evidence retrieval and refresh workflows
- Integration with internal identity, notification, and records management services

## Workflow Expansion

- More advanced SLA management, escalation policies, and management reporting
- Cross-case knowledge reuse and portfolio-level audit oversight
- Playbook templating by audit type, jurisdiction, and question class
- Configurable operating models for different business units and review chains

## Package and Submission Expansion

- Additional export formats and regulator-specific packaging templates
- Submission-ready validation packs tailored to authority expectations
- Controlled handoff into external filing or regulator submission channels where appropriate

## Intelligence and Learning Expansion

- Evaluation frameworks for retrieval quality, strategy quality, and draft usefulness
- Feedback-driven improvements to prompt libraries, ranking logic, and workflow templates
- Outcome-informed recommendation engines for precedent and evidence planning

## Platform Expansion

- Multi-tenant or multi-business-unit deployment model
- Advanced governance dashboards for AI operations, usage, and control effectiveness
- Scalable semantic data layer and evidence catalog with richer metadata normalization
